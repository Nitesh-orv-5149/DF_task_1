Game Instructions

Game Setup

Board: Three concentric hexagonal circuits with numbered edge weights.

Players: Each player has 4 Titans (Red and Blue).

Timer: Each player gets 2 minutes total game time.

Game Phases

1. Placement Phase

  • Players take turns placing Titans on the outermost circuit.
  • When the outer circuit is filled, the middle circuit unlocks.
  • When the middle circuit is filled, the inner circuit unlocks.

2. Movement Phase

  • After all Titans are placed, players move one Titan per turn.
  • Titans can only move along edges to adjacent empty nodes.
  • A Titan surrounded by opponent Titans is removed from play.

Scoring

  • Points are earned by controlling edges (when both connected nodes are occupied by your Titans).
  • Points equal to the edge's weight are added to your score.
  • If a Titan moves away from a controlled edge, points are deducted.

Winning Conditions

  • When a player's timer expires, they lose.
  • When the innermost hexagon is fully occupied, the game ends and the player with the highest score wins.
